My First Heading

My name is Vanesa Marar. I was born in Khmelnitsky, Ukraine. Being the oldest, my parents relied on me achieving things they were not able to achieve as they were growing up in Ukraine and moved to America. I love mathematics, science, history, writing, reading, badminton, and art. I am a devoted Christian and I am considered to be very conservative. It usually plays a role when forming opinions, but I still maintain an open-mind while still grasping onto my beliefs. I am good with advice and listening, being referred to as allocentric. I am always very exuberant and bubbly, since bad moods is something I never experience, so people often look up to me to lighten up the atmosphere. Being an extrovert, I display some tranquilness when it seems appropriate to revent any presumptuous or immature behavior, which eventuates in others questioning my classification of myself.
